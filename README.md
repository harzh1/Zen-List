# Zen-List
Welcome to Zen List, your serene companion for efficient task management. Embrace the tranquility of streamlined organization and effortlessly manage your to-do list. Zen List combines simplicity with powerful features to help you achieve your tasks with calm focus.

#Key Features
Serenity in Tasks: Experience a zen-like approach to task management.
Effortless Organization: Easily create, edit, and prioritize tasks for a seamless workflow.
Focus Mode: Enhance your productivity with a dedicated focus mode.
Priority Sorting: Prioritize tasks to stay on top of your most important goals.
User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design for a smooth and delightful user experience.
#Getting Started
Installation: Clone the repository and follow the installation guide in the documentation.
Create Your Tasks: Start by adding your tasks and prioritize them for optimal efficiency.
Enjoy Zen Productivity: Immerse yourself in a calm and focused approach to task management.
